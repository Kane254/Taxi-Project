1-Day Shimba Hills Game Reserve Safari

A unique coastal safari experience, home to the rare Sable Antelope and stunning Sheldrick Falls.

From $120 per person

Detailed Routine: Full Day Trip

Perfect for guests staying in Diani, offering a chance to see rare wildlife and coastal forest scenery in a single day.

Morning: Game Drive & Falls Hike

  • **7:00 AM:** Pick up from your Diani/Mombasa hotel.
  • **8:00 AM:** Arrive at Shimba Hills, enter the reserve, and begin the morning game drive targeting the Sable Antelope.
  • **10:00 AM:** Guided nature walk and hike down to the spectacular **Sheldrick Falls**.
  • **12:30 AM:** Return to the lodge for lunch (not included).

Afternoon Trip & Final Drive

  • **10:45 AM:** Depart from Diani/Mombasa Hotel.
  • **12:00 PM:** Arrive at Shimba Hills Game Reserve and begin the afternoon game drive.
  • **2:00 PM:** Break for a packed lunch (not included) at a scenic spot within the reserve.
  • **2:30 PM:** Guided nature walk and hike down to the spectacular **Sheldrick Falls**.
  • **4:30 PM:** Depart the reserve and head back to your coastal accommodation.

Evening: Return

**5:00 PM:** Depart Shimba Hills and transfer back to your coastal accommodation.

  • ✅ Private round-trip transport from your hotel.
  • ✅ Professional driver.
  • ✅ Park entry fees (for vehicle and passenger).
  • ✅ Guided hike to Sheldrick Falls by a KWS guide.
