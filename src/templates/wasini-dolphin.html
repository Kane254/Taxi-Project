Wasini Dolphin & Snorkel Tour

Morning boat trip to Kisite-Mpunguti Marine Park for dolphin spotting and snorkeling, followed by Wasini Island culture and fresh seafood lunch.

From $75 per person

What to Expect on the Tour

This full-day excursion departs early and combines marine wildlife watching, snorkeling on vibrant reefs, a local seafood lunch on Wasini Island, and a chance to learn about island life and optional visits to nearby historical sites.

Pickup & Transfer to Shimoni

  • Early morning pickup from your hotel in Diani or Mombasa and transfer to the KWS/boat jetty at Shimoni.
  • Brief on-board safety and park rules; payment of marine park fees if not pre-arranged.

Dolphin Spotting

  • Head out to Kisite-Mpunguti Marine Park where guides look for pods of playful dolphins often seen close to the boat.
  • Time for photographs and observing dolphin behavior from a safe distance.

Snorkelling at Kisite Reefs

  • Stop at designated snorkeling sites to swim among coral gardens and colorful reef fish. Snorkel gear and life jackets are provided.
  • Guides assist non-swimmers and highlight marine species and reef health.

Wasini Island Lunch & Village Walk

  • Sail to Wasini Island for a fresh seafood lunch (often the day's catch) served by local vendors.
  • After lunch, take a guided walk through the village to learn about local culture and livelihoods.

Optional Shimoni Slave Caves Visit

  • Optional short visit to the historic Shimoni slave caves (if time and interest) to learn about the area's difficult history. This is handled sensitively and respectfully.

Return & Drop-off

  • Return to the Shimoni jetty and transfer back to your hotel in the late afternoon.

What's Included?

  • ✅ Pickup and drop-off from Diani/Mombasa (local pickup area)
  • ✅ Boat transport to Kisite-Mpunguti Marine Park and Wasini Island
  • ✅ Life jackets, snorkel gear, and safety briefing
  • ✅ Guided dolphin watching and snorkeling session
  • ✅ Seafood lunch on Wasini Island
  • ❌ National park fees (payable locally unless pre-paid)
  • ❌ Personal purchases and gratuities
